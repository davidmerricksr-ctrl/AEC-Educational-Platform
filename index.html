About
Educational Purpose
An independent, non-commercial resource for learning about economic crime.

This platform brings together educational content from leading organisations and US government public domain resources to help students, researchers, and professionals understand the landscape of financial crime.

All content is sourced from publicly available materials — including US government publications (public domain under 17 U.S.C. § 105) and UK public interest resources.

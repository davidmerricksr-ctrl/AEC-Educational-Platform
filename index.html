About This Platform
Education as the First Line of Defence

Economic crime encompasses a spectrum of illegal activities — fraud, money laundering, bribery, corruption, terrorist financing, and cybercrime — that threaten the stability of financial systems, undermine public trust, and inflict enormous harm on individuals and communities around the world.

This educational platform exists to help students, researchers, compliance professionals, and the general public understand the mechanisms, typologies, and societal consequences of economic crime. By building awareness and critical knowledge, we can collectively strengthen our defences against these threats.

All information presented here is curated from publicly available, authoritative sources and is intended purely as a learning resource. We encourage users to explore the referenced organisations directly for current guidance and professional advice.

Educational Purpose Statement

This platform is provided strictly for educational and non-commercial purposes. It is designed to:

  • Raise awareness of economic crime typologies
  • Highlight the societal and human cost of financial crime
  • Provide a learning resource for students and professionals
  • Signpost authoritative organisations and resources
  • Inspire the next generation of financial crime professionals

This platform does not provide legal, financial, or regulatory advice.

Launch Reflections: Impact, Awareness & First Steps

The first interview — why economic crime's invisible harm matters most, who this site is really for, and the one thing every visitor should walk away understanding.

📝 Key Takeaways

The invisible impact problem. Economic crime doesn't leave visible scars, which makes raising awareness harder — but no less critical. The site leads with impact metrics to bridge that gap.

Bridge, not guilt trip. The goal isn't emotional manipulation — it's practical: show people what economic crime looks like, how it works, and how they can join the fight.

Typologies as the entry point. For newcomers, the typologies section is the best first click — a structured overview of how different crimes are perpetrated and disrupted.

The Ops Simulator. A hands-on tool letting visitors experience what an analyst's workday actually looks like — entirely AI-built, not based on any real vendor system.

"Tell Your Story" feature. A new idea born in this interview — providing an anonymous interface for victims and witnesses to share their experiences. Coming soon.
